Corporate Structure

Oil City Ready Mix Ltd. operates as an independent entity within a group of companies comprising separately incorporated businesses with distinct ownership structures, management teams, and governance frameworks.

Collectively, the group is engaged in a diverse range of business activities, including a concrete ready-mix manufacturing operation, a property management business, and a trucking company specializing in the transportation of materials to and from project sites. This diversity enables the businesses to operate with focused expertise across different sectors while contributing to a broader network of industry experience.

While the companies may collaborate and align on certain operational matters where beneficial, each organization retains full responsibility for its own strategic direction, operational decision-making, and financial performance. This approach supports flexibility and responsiveness at the individual company level, while also allowing for the exchange of industry knowledge and operational efficiencies where appropriate.

Concrete quantities provided by this calculator are estimates based solely on user inputs and are for guidance purposes only. Actual quantities may vary depending on site conditions and execution.

For surface pours, variations in subgrade levelness, preparation, and formwork accuracy can impact the final volume required. Uneven or irregular surfaces may lead to higher or lower concrete consumption than estimated.

For pile or cylindrical pours, quantities assume accurate excavation to specified dimensions. In practice, over-excavation, soil instability, or irregular borehole shapes may affect actual concrete usage.

Users should allow for reasonable contingencies and verify quantities with site professionals prior to ordering. No liability is assumed for any variance between estimated and actual quantities.
