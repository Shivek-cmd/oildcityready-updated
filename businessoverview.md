## Corporate Structure

Oil City Ready Mix Ltd. operates as an independent entity within a group of separately incorporated companies under **Oil City Holding Ltd.**, each with distinct ownership, management, and governance.

| Company                   | Role                                     |
|---------------------------|------------------------------------------|
| **Oil City Ready Mix Ltd. (OCRM)** | Concrete Ready-Mix Manufacturing |
| **2687531 Alberta Ltd.**  | Property Management                      |
| **2689073 Alberta Ltd.**  | Trucking & Material Transportation       |

While the companies may collaborate operationally where beneficial, each retains full responsibility for its own strategic direction, decisions, and financial performance.
